  1. At the end of every year (round), each team will send out 1 participant to answer a MCQ question on property investment. The participant will first need to answer a math question. The fastest participant that manages to answer the math question correctly will be given the priority to answer the MCQ question and subsequently if they answer wrongly, the next in sequence will get the chance to answer. The round will end once any of the participant answers the MCQ question correctly. If none of the participants manages to answer the MCQ question correctly, nothing will happen and the game continues. The winner of every round will get an additional capital of S$30 million. The rest of the teams will have their properties value decreases by 20%.

 

 

To take note:

The number of years (rounds) is not fixed which means that the game could end in any year (round) even when the properties chosen are not fully build yet. Thus, do plan wisely and invest prudently!
