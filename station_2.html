Bras Basah MRT Station Exit C

Instructions

The game will adopt a Snake and Ladder concept where a member of each team of three will be inside the grid and making progress though the grid by throwing the dice. The number of steps will correspond to the number of dice rolled each time.

The other two members of the three-man team would be solving riddles that if solved, will award them with Mystery Box that will give them money to spend throughout their life.

At the start, each team will have a certain amount of money, e.g. 1000, which they can use to purchase assets, such as insurance or securities or property

Each team will start at the age of 18. Each dice rolled means 2 years will have passed by. The age will be significant as at certain age, inevitable events will occur that will affect their finances.Each time a player steps on a tile, they will be faced with either one of the following:

  1. Event that presents the group with a choice that affect their finances
    1. Whether or not to buy an insurance which will cost $100 and give a one-time chance to offset the effect of a possible injury in the future. Only the cost price (i.e. $100) will be revealed. The actual worth of the asset of $300 in a few years’ time will not be disclosed.
    2. Whether or not to buy a property for $300 the value of which will multiply by 10% every year until the time limit is reached
    3. Whether or not to perform unethical business practice that will bring present cash inflow but may risk having to pay damages in case someday the player steps on a court tile which will inevitably uncover the player’s unethical actions and render the player liable to pay damages.
  1. Inescapable event (Positive/negative outcomes)
    1. Accident that will cost some amount of money
    2. Lottery winning
  1. No events at all

 

Each action will affect the group’s finances

  • Accidents
  • Purchase of securities
  • Illness
  • Insurance-related
  • Education-related
  • Marriage-related
    •

 

Mystery boxes can be acquired if the remaining 2 players could solve as many riddles as possible → write answers on a paper, submit at the end of the game: (crossword, MCQ types of question)

 

Instructions for players:

  • Person on each group allowed to use phone for calculator purpose, not research
  • Person throw dice and decide to move diagonally, horizontally or vertically.
  • Riddles: The more correct answers you get, the higher points you will earn
  • Each group may ask the helper for their current wealth, but get 2000 deducted
  • The time limit is 15 minutes.
  • The goal is to accumulate highest amount of wealth compared to other groups
  • Each group may manually calculate their current accumulated wealth on a piece of paper or ask helpers a maximum of 5 times their current accumulated wealth. Below is a sample of the format you can use.

Scoring System: 

Since around 3-5 groups will be at the station at any point in time, the groups’ points will be ranked against the others playing at the same time. The group with the highest point will earn 5000, and the ones ranked below will get 4000, 3000, 2000, or 1000 correspondingly.
