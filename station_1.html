Instructions

This game has a total of 4 segments, 2 competitive and 2 non-competitive.

Stage 1: MCQs (Competitive)

There will be a total of 10 MCQ questions (3 options). Each team is given a set of “1/2/3” signs. The teams are given 10 seconds to answer each question by raising their signs. First team to raise their sign will be allowed to answer first.

Stage 2: Memory Card Game (Non-competitive)

This game is played by the participants within their team. There is a total of 3 mins allocated to this stage. Participants from each team are to open only 2 cards at one time. The 2 cards can be removed from the layout only when they are identical.

There are 3 sets of cards with varying difficulty (basic, intermediate, advanced). Each difficulty level will have different number of pairs of cards. The team will choose one difficulty level to complete. Points awarded will be based on the amount of time taken. If the team is able to complete one set within 3 mins, they can move on to another set and continue playing until the full 3 mins is up. The points awarded will be cumulative.

Stage 4: Charades (Competitive/Non-competitive)

This game involves one representative of the team to act out the charades presented by the game helper. His/her teammates are then required to guess the corresponding charades. The duration for this game is 3 minutes. Points awarded will be based on the amount of correct answer given. If there are words that you stumble upon, you can request to skip.
