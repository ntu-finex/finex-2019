Instructions

  1. First, the group participants will get virtual 100 sgd. There will be 6 boxes. In each of the boxes, there’s a type of currency.

    There will be 6 boxes. In each of the boxes, there’s a type of currency. (Euro, US Dollar, British Pound etc). The contents of the boxes will not be disclosed to the participants. Through the completion of tasks, a group can get some ‘tips’ from the game masters to deduce the type of currencies hidden in the boxes. The first team to complete the task will get all the tips (5 at maximum), the second team will get four and vice versa. With the ‘tips’, they have to make their final decision and purchase the box which they think contains the currency which has the highest value.

    Tasks Details:

    1) A certain picture that is taken somewhere nearby the game location (For example, a picture of a building) will be shown to the participants. They will have to search for that thing that is shown in the picture and take a picture of it with their mobile devices and show it to the game master to complete the task.

    2) Puzzle task: A picture of the FINEX logo will be divided into multiple puzzle pieces and the participants will have to assemble the puzzle pieces to form the complete picture to proceed to the next task.

    3) Currency Symbol Task: The participants will have to draw 5 random currency symbols. (For example: The Japanese yen symbol) They will have to guess the countries that use the symbols and pin them accordingly to the matching countries on a big world map with thumbtacks.
